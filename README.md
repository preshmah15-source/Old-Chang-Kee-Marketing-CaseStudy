Old Chang Kee Brand Refresh (MARK213)

 Overview
Marketing case study on **Old Chang Kee**, a Singaporean snack brand.  
Focused on consumer insights, personas, segmentation, and a **brand refresh strategy**.

---

 Objectives
- Apply **4Ps** and environmental analysis.  
- Conduct consumer interviews and build personas.  
- Propose digital engagement and healthier product options.  
- Recommend brand refresh actions aligned with modern trends.

---

 Tools & Methods
- Consumer interviews (students, mall staff).  
- Personas: Impulse Buyer, Group Provider, Novelty Seeker.  
- Marketing frameworks: STP, 4Ps.  
- Digital strategy: TikTok, Instagram, QR codes.

---

 My Contributions
- Developed **consumer insights** and personas.  
- Proposed **digital engagement strategy** (TikTok/IG content, QR packaging).  
- Helped draft **brand refresh actions** (healthier snacks, combo boxes).

---

 Key Recommendations
- Introduce **baked/vegetarian options**.  
- Launch **group bundle deals** for students.  
- Enhance **digital visibility** with SEO + QR codes.  
- Strengthen **social media presence** with snackable content.
